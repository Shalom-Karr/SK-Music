SK Music · Connection Test

Checks whether your network — a filter like Techloq, school/work Wi-Fi, or a phone content-filter/whitelist — is blocking anything SK Music needs, especially music playback. Nothing plays out loud. When it finishes, tap Share log (or Copy / Download) and send it to whoever set up the filter.
Run this on the device that has the problem, on the Wi-Fi/data it normally uses.
